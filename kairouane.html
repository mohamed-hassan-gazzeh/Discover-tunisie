A City of Faith and Heritage

Kairouan is one of the most important Islamic cities in North Africa. Founded in the 7th century, it is famous for its sacred monuments, traditional medina, and rich cultural heritage. The city is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Things to Do in Kairouan

  • Visit the Great Mosque of Kairouan
  • Explore the old medina
  • Discover traditional carpets and crafts
