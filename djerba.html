An Island of Culture and Relaxation

Djerba is a beautiful Mediterranean island known for its sandy beaches, whitewashed villages, and peaceful atmosphere. It is also famous for its cultural diversity, traditional markets, and historic El Ghriba Synagogue.

Things to Do in Djerba

  • Relax on the sandy beaches
  • Visit El Ghriba Synagogue
  • Explore Houmt Souk markets
