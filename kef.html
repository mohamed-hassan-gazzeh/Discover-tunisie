A Mountain City of History

El Kef is a historic mountain city in northwestern Tunisia, known for its dramatic cliffs, Andalusian heritage, and rich artistic traditions. Its old town offers stunning views and a peaceful atmosphere away from the coast.

Things to Do in El Kef

  • Visit the Kasbah of El Kef
  • Explore the old medina
  • Enjoy panoramic mountain views
